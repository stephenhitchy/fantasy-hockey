LIVE MODE + PROJECTION CALIBRATION V1

LIVE RUNTIME
- Changed the local development runtime from historical replay to live NHL mode.
- Removed the configured 2026-01-10 historical scoring date.
- The historical-mode banner therefore no longer appears during npm start.
- Production was already configured for live mode and remains protected.
- Local developer tools remain enabled until the visual-interface cleanup.

PROJECTION VERSION 6
- Forwards: manager-facing multiplier changed from 0.90 to 0.95.
- Defensemen: manager-facing multiplier changed from 0.88 to 0.93.
- Goalie units: manager-facing multiplier changed from 0.85 to 0.90.
- The raw projection model is unchanged; only the final intentional
  conservatism applied before freezing a window projection was narrowed.
- The target is to move from roughly 90% under-projected outcomes toward
  roughly 70%, while still making overperformance more common than misses.
- Exact percentages will vary and should be reviewed with Projection Accuracy.

DATA SAFETY
- Shared projection version advanced from 5 to 6.
- Version 5 snapshots are treated as stale and regenerate for new windows.
- Existing frozen cycle and playoff projections remain immutable.
- Completed projection-accuracy records remain associated with their original
  version, allowing Version 5 and Version 6 results to be compared.
