BANKED PLAYOFF WINDOWS V1 — TEST PLAN

A. DETERMINISTIC SIMULATOR (AVAILABLE NOW)
1. Sign in as commissioner.
2. Open the Playoff Bracket page.
3. Click "Test Banked Windows".
4. Confirm the banner says all seven checks passed.

THE SEVEN CHECKS VERIFY
- Opening-round teams use Window 1.
- Seeds with byes keep Window 1 banked.
- Bye teams enter the semifinals using Window 1.
- Opening-round teams advance using Window 2.
- Opening-round losers route to the fifth-place game.
- No owner/window pair is used twice.
- One matchup can use different window numbers on its two sides.

The simulator performs no NHL requests and no Firestore writes.

B. FRESH HISTORICAL PLAYOFF TEST
Use a new test league or reset the test league before playoffs begin.

1. Finish the final regular-season cycle.
2. Open the first playoff round as commissioner.
3. In Firestore, confirm bank documents exist at:
   /leagues/{leagueId}/playoffWindowBanks/{ownerId}/windows/window-1
4. Confirm every seeded team has Window 1, including teams with a bye.
5. Confirm only teams actually playing the opening round have Window 1 marked
   assigned to a matchup.
6. Open a playoff matchup and allow historical scoring to load.
7. Confirm Window 1 progress updates without duplicate game IDs.
8. Once an opening-round bank completes, confirm Window 2 is created even if
   another roster slot or matchup is still pending.
9. Complete the opening round.
10. Open the next round and inspect the bracket:
    - a bye seed should display Window 1
    - its opening-round opponent should display Window 2
11. Confirm already-completed Window 2 points appear immediately.
12. Confirm opening-round losers use Window 2 in the fifth-place game.
13. Reload and open a second tab; confirm no window is assigned twice and scores
    do not duplicate.

C. QUEUED MOVE TEST
1. Queue a replacement for an active roster slot during a playoff bank.
2. Confirm the outgoing asset remains in the current bank.
3. Complete that slot's six-game bank.
4. Confirm the incoming asset appears in the next bank.
5. Confirm the outgoing asset enters waivers only at activation.
6. Confirm the previous bank remains permanently tied to the outgoing asset.

D. PROJECTION FREEZE TEST
1. Record a player's frozen projection in Window 1.
2. Refresh scoring and regenerate shared projections.
3. Confirm Window 1 does not change.
4. When Window 2 opens, confirm it receives its own frozen projection.

E. EXPECTED LIMITATION OF HISTORICAL REPLAY
The prior-season test mode processes finalized data rapidly. It does not mimic
the real-time spacing of NHL games, but the deterministic simulator verifies the
routing and mixed-window behavior independently of dates.
